About Riglify

Riglify was created to help Roblox creators easily import avatars, bundles, models, and accessories into Prisma3D and other animation software.

Many existing tools out there stopped working, became outdated, or felt unsafe to use. Riglify aims to provide a cleaner, modern, and community-focused alternative.

I was inspired by faizda.my.id until the site shut down permanently. This project does something similar, but mostly focuses on accessibility, creator freedom, and mobile-friendly tools.
